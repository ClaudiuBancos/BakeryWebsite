About

Hi, we're Claudiu and Celeste Bancos. This is our bakery website, where we share pictures of baked goods that we've made. We make wedding cakes, custom cakes for events, and baked goods for all occasions! We're located in Bethlehem, PA but are willing to travel throughout the Lehigh Valley and beyond! When we're not baking, we enjoy programming, playing board games, and spending time with friends and family. We're also very involved in our church.
